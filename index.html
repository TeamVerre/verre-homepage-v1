Consistent

It has never been easier to start driving for a ride-sharing platform, but it's also never been more difficult to find a consistent, efficient ride home. Ride-sharing is a solution for the average customer but it's no replacement for the institution of luxury car service in New York City. Verre exists to bring that trusted institution to the 21st century with a suite of scheduling tools, sophisticated smart dispatching, a lightweight mobile app, a cost-saving and automated backend, and the highest qualified chauffers vetted personally by our founder.

Most of all, we understand your employees and their lifestyle, and know that they are worthy of a car service tailored just for them.

Undivided

Your average black car driver is more worried about not making mistakes than about striving for excellence. However, Verre is not your not your average car service - our proprietary platform provides luxury cars and experienced chauffers to select business clients more efficiently than ever before, increasing speed and reducing overhead. Don't settle for drivers that transport anybody with an app- your employees deserve chauffers happy to wait for them with cars matched by a smart automated dispatch system.

We only work with a few select companies so our chauffeurs are ready for you on your schedule, not theirs. Our chauffeurs are devoted to building and maintaining exclusive relationships with specific companies so they understand your precise needs and will never leave you hanging.

Affordable

Due to our lean profile and our hardworking algorithms, Verre offers luxury car service at frugal prices. Riding in comfort and style shouldn't break the bank, but the lack of innovation in this sphere has led to complacency, corruption, and nepotism in the black car industry. Verre provides qualified chauffeurs for less than the price of a driver.
